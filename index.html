About Me

Hello there! My name is George Hernandez, a 31-year-old licensed massage therapist with a passion for making the transition to software development. Originally from San Antonio, I made the exciting move to Austin back in 2010. Outside of work, you'll often find me indulging in a variety of hobbies. I'm an avid player of both tennis and pickleball, constantly seeking to improve my game. I prioritize my fitness by hitting the gym regularly and enjoy any opportunity to be in or around water. Gaming and watching anime also hold special places in my heart, and I'm always up for an adventure. Whether it's exploring a new city or hitting the slopes! I'm a proud father of two pugs and a French bulldog, each equiped with ridiculous personalities. Compassion, honesty, and respect serve as guiding principles in both my personal and professional life. I firmly believe in treating others with the kindness and dignity they deserve, while staying true to oneself. One of my greatest strengths lies in my communication skills and my knack for quickly grasping new concepts. These qualities make me confident that I would be an asset to any team or company.

My journey towards software development really began after an untimely gym injury, and being forced to take a lot of down time. Determined not waste it, I began to reconsider the longevity of body work, and made the decision to look for something less physical. Going off my observation of the tech boom and my own prior knowledge from gaming, I decided to explore onliine computer courses and stumbled upon coding. After immersing myself in a few free Python tutorials, I found myself hooked. The thrill of learning something new and the sense of challenge it brought really reaffirmed my decision to pursue it further. I embarked on this new chapter and have enrolled in the UT coding bootcamp. Though in the beginning stages, I am finding myself being challenged like never before. However this has only sharpened my determination to pursue my newfound passion for software development, as well as reminded me I can do anything I put my mind too. I am eager to bring my unique blend of skills, experiences, and enthusiasm to the world of technology, and I can't wait to see where this journey takes me.
